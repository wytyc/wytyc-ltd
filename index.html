Digital Wealth Should be
Safely Passed On.

The Property (Digital Assets etc) Act 2025 confirms that crypto, NFTs and domains are personal property under English Law. With this change, digital assets now have clearer legal protection, making it much easier to secure your property and manage your estates.

The UK is entering the largest intergenerational wealth transfer in modern history with billions in digital assets held by individuals and companies who now require secure, legally compliant succession systems.

The Succession Crisis

The Lost Key Crisis

Digital wallets have no "password reset." If a holder passes away without a cryptographic plan, their wealth is permanently erased from the economy.

The Legal Gap

Solicitors hold the legal authority but lack the technical tooling to interact with blockchain vaults or handle private keys securely.

The Compliance Trap

Executors are now legally liable for digital assets under UK law, yet lack the "Master Map" required to locate or secure them.
